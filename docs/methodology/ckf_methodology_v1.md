# CKF Methodology v1

## Experience Record

Capture experience as structured, source-aware records in the MEL. Include context, dates, responsibilities, outcomes, and evidence where available.

## Capability Extraction

Translate experience records into demonstrated capabilities in the CPIL. Preserve links back to the originating experience and supporting evidence.

## Capability Assessment

Assess capability patterns in the CCA. Identify strengths, transferability, depth, limits, and career alignment without adding unsupported claims.

## Presentation Generation

Use the RGF to generate targeted career artifacts from governed upstream material. Adapt emphasis and wording for the opportunity while preserving traceability.
