# CKF Governance Rules v1

## MEL as Authoritative Source

The Master Experience Ledger is the authoritative source for experience records. Claims about experience should trace back to the MEL.

## Downstream Artifacts Are Derivative

CPIL, CCA, RGF, and targeted career artifacts derive from upstream records. They should not introduce unsupported facts.

## Evidence Over Memory When Available

When evidence exists, it should be preferred over recollection. Memory may be used as a provisional source when clearly marked.

## Versioned Revisions

Meaningful artifact changes should be versioned so revisions remain reviewable and explainable.

## No Unsupported Inflation

Artifacts must not exaggerate seniority, scope, impact, ownership, credentials, or outcomes beyond available support.

## Separate Fact, Interpretation, and Presentation

Facts record what happened. Interpretation explains what the facts demonstrate. Presentation adapts supported facts and interpretations for a specific audience or opportunity.
